How to borrow book

1. Go to Homepage
2. Click on "Scan QR Code" button
3. Choose camera preference
4. Scan QR code of the book you wish to borrow
5. Click "Borrow Now!"

How to return book

1. Place book back in community library
2. Go to Homepage
3. Click on book you wish to return
4. Click "Returned"
